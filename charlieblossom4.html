HARDLY WORKING

Ned Williams

Sharon Atkins

I got great satisfaction from the paper rounds, far more than going to a library or hanging around the city room.

I’d go down and fill up the cart and that fucking thing’s heavy.

I’d have to push it and it would take strength and I’d sweat. It’s like 250 or 300 papers to go around on each edition.

I liked it ‘cause I sweated and I got into conversations with people.

I’d get done and I would say, “I did something.”

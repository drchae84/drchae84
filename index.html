Sharon Atkins

My fantasies all spring at the paper was getting a machine gun and coming in and shooting them.

Getting psychedelic hallucinogens and putting them in their drinks.

Getting a gun and walk into the editor’s office and shooting him. Maybe pointing the gun at him first and say, “Okay, how do you face your death?

I saw a Japanese movie once where two guys met their deaths in two different ways. That’s the kind of fantasies I had, cutting ‘em up with knives.
